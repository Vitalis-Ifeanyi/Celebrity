Davido has six children; three boys (one of whom is now deceased), and three girls. The singer confirmed his marriage to his partner Chioma Rowland in March 2023.

On October 31, 2022, Peoples Gazette newspaper reported the death of Davido's oldest son and third child David Ifeanyi Adeleke Jr., who drowned in a swimming pool at his father's Banana Island residence; he was three years old at the time of his death. Police questioned members of Davido's household staff. Eight domestic workers were invited to the police station for questioning after one had reported the drowning accident to the police station. Following the police investigation, six workers were released and two remained in detention.

On June 25, 2024, Davido married his long-term partner, Chioma Rowland at Harbour Point in Victoria Island, Lagos. The event was attended by guests including Olusegun Obasanjo, Babajide Sanwo-Olu, Don Jazzy, Kcee, Chike, Zlatan, Ebuka Obi-Uchendu, Fireboy DML, VeryDarkMan, Iyabo Ojo, Olamide, Mayorkun, Nasboi, King Sunny Ade and Ademola Adeleke. He was styled by Deangelictouchstylist, also designers like Lucky Enemuo and Ugo Monye contributed to the wedding looks.
